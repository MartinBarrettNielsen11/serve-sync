# Invariants


## Session Invariants
- A session must never exceed its maximum player capacity
- A booking may not be canceled free of charge within 24 hours of the session start time


## Club Invariants
- A club may not operate more courts than permitted by its subscription


## Court Invariants
- A court cannot have more sessions than the subscription allows
- A court cannot have two or more overlapping sessions


## Instructor Invariants
- A instructor may not be assigned to overlapping sessions


## Player Invariants
- A player cannot book overlapping sessions


## Subscription Invariants
- A subscription cannot have more clubs than the subscription allows
